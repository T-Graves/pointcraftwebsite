Pointcraft Custom Counter Tops was founded in 1999 by Charlie Loomis and has rapidly grown ever since. We are a family-owned business that serves Southern Wisconsin including areas such as Madison, Dodgeville, Baraboo, Reedsburg, Richland Center, Sparta, Janesville, and many more. We work closely with our customers to design and fabricate counter tops for any layout.

We are located just outside of Hill Point Wisconsin.
